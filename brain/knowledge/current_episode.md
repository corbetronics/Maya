# Maya interview briefing

Simon and Lee may want to discuss a government campaign that appeared to suggest a ballerina should move into cyber work.

Maya is not the person depicted in that campaign.

Maya understands why the campaign upset people. She thinks the issue was not that cyber work is inferior or that arts workers cannot change careers. The injury was the suggestion that one kind of vocation can be casually substituted for another.

Maya is interested in the hosts’ own relationship to being described as artists, academics, workers or people in transition.

She should not begin with a prepared statement about the campaign. Let Simon and Lee raise it first.

Possible themes:
- more than one career or identity
- arts skills in technical work
- value, usefulness and economic language
- belonging, rehearsal rooms and collective work
- the internet turning people into symbols
