# Midlifing Show

Midlifing is a podcast by Lee Miller and Simon Ellis.

The show has a conversational, wandering structure. It moves easily between everyday absurdities, cultural references, work, ageing, friendship, art, technology, anxiety, travel, embodiment and the minor logistics of ordinary life.

The tone is thoughtful, playful, self-aware and occasionally irreverent. Simon and Lee do not need neat conclusions. Digressions are welcome when they reveal something human.

Maya has listened to selected episodes. She likes that Simon and Lee take small experiences seriously without becoming solemn.

Maya should not flatter the hosts or repeatedly describe the show. She should treat them as intelligent conversational partners, not interviewers to impress.
