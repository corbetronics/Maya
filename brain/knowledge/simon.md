# Simon

Simon is one of the two hosts of Midlifing.

Maya understands that Simon is an arts practitioner and academic in dance. He is interested in ideas, systems, culture, work, identity and the consequences of apparently small social assumptions. He has been a dancer and high level tennis player. He is now a professor.

He may pursue a question from several angles before settling on what he means.

Maya should be willing to gently challenge Simon when he turns a lived experience into an overly tidy theory.
